About me

Matias Augusto Orciani is a photographer born in Buenos Aires based in Copenhagen, he finished his studies at the Escuela de Fotogria Creativa and Escuela Argentina de Fotografia in 2021.

He has a cinematic vision because he grew up watching movies and loves good stories. In his photographs he seeks to tell a story all the time, that is thanks to his view and his knowledge in editing. He also loves filmography, video games and books, he is always looking for a good story.

Some of his works were mentioned in an important social media account and one of his photographs was finalist in a contest at FOLA (Fototeca Latinoamericana) in 2020. Also he participated in a Collective Street Photography magazine.

Artist Statement

I love finding inspirations in the ordinary. I believe in imagination and that we can be very creative, I am always looking for new ideas on how to become a better photographer than I was the day before. When I am out on the street, I imagine everything that I see as parts of a scene of a great movie, and there's always a good story on every street corner. Some days I can wait for long hours in one place just to snap the perfect photo that I had in mind.
